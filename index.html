About

Where AI meets
social good.

Sealai is a product studio and strategic advisory focused on the intersection of artificial intelligence and social impact. We build our own products and partner with organizations that are using technology to drive meaningful, lasting change.

Sealai is founded by Yu-Ann Madan — an AI product and strategy leader who currently serves as Head of Impact Hours at OpenAI. Her career spans Google, McKinsey, leading venture studios, and global conservation organizations — bringing a blend of rigorous analytical thinking, product intuition, and deep technical fluency shaped by a graduate certificate in AI/ML from Stanford.

We believe the most transformative products sit at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and real-world need. Sealai exists to build and launch at that intersection.
